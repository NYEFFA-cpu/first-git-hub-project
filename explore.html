🏛️ explore campus

✨ six places · six stories · University of Copenhagen

📚 Library 📖

A silent sanctuary with over 1.2 million volumes, rare manuscripts, and cozy reading nooks. The heart of academic life.

💡
Fun fact: The library tower has a hidden observatory used for celestial navigation in the 19th century.
☕ Cafeteria 🍽️

Modern, bright dining hall serving sustainable Nordic food. Coffee, pastries, and daily specials made from local produce.

🌱
Fun fact: The cafeteria’s rooftop garden supplies herbs and edible flowers for the dishes — and it’s student-maintained.
⚽ Football Pitch 🏟️

Floodlit artificial turf pitch, home to the university teams. Open for casual games, tournaments, and late-night training.

🏆
Fun fact: In 2023, the pitch hosted the first “Nordic Scholar Cup” — and the staff team beat the professors 3–2!
💻 Computer Labs 🖥️

State-of-the-art labs with VR stations, 3D printers, and high-performance computing clusters open 24/7 for students.

🧠
Fun fact: One of the labs houses a vintage supercomputer from 1985 — it still runs a weather simulation every month.
🎓 Student Centre 🧑‍🎓

The social hub: study lounges, student services, club offices, and a cozy fireplace lounge. The heart of student life.

🗺️
Fun fact: The Centre’s main hall was once a train station waiting room — the original ticket booth is now a info desk.
🚀 Innovation Hub ⚡

A dynamic co-working space for startups, makers, and dreamers. Equipped with prototyping tools and mentorship labs.

🔬
Fun fact: The Hub’s first spin-off created a drone that plants trees — and it’s now used in reforestation projects across Denmark.

📖 event details

🏃 Sports Day

A full day of athletic competitions, team sports, and fun activities. Whether you're a serious athlete or just want to have fun, there's something for everyone. Events include football, basketball, relay races, and a tug-of-war challenge. Prizes for the winning teams!

💻 Coding Hackathon

A 12-hour coding marathon where teams compete to build innovative projects. Work on real-world challenges, network with industry professionals, and win exciting prizes. All skill levels welcome — from beginners to experts. Food and drinks provided throughout the day.

🎬 Movie Night

Cozy up in the Student Centre Auditorium for a classic movie screening. This month's feature is a student-voted cult favorite. Popcorn, snacks, and drinks available. Bring your friends and enjoy a relaxing evening of cinema and community.

🌍 Cultural Festival

Celebrate the diversity of our campus community! Enjoy international food, traditional music and dance performances, art exhibitions, and cultural workshops. A vibrant day of sharing and learning about cultures from around the world.

🎤 Talent Show

Showcase your talent on stage — singing, dancing, comedy, magic, or anything in between. A night of entertainment and celebration of creativity. Audience voting determines the winners, with special prizes for the top performers.

💼 Career Fair

Connect with top employers from various industries. Bring your CV, practice your elevator pitch, and explore internship and job opportunities. Workshops on interview skills and resume writing will also be available throughout the day.
